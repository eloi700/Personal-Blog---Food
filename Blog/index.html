LET'S EAT!

"Let's eat!" In the Filipino language, it's "kain tayo" (ka-in ta-yo), an invitation for you to share the food in front of others. An attitude of sharing no matter the food is enough or not.

Never underestimate the power of good food. Eating delicious food can be a life-changing experience. - Shon Mehta
